# Resume Templates for High School & College Students

Starting your career is exciting, but writing your first resume can be daunting. Whether you need a **resume template for high school students** or a **resume template for college student** applications, the strategy is similar: leverage your potential.

## For High School Students
Possessing limited work experience? No problem.
*   **Focus on Education**: GPA (if above 3.0), relevant coursework, and clubs.
*   **Volunteer Work**: This counts as experience!
*   **Skills**: Computer skills, languages, and soft skills like teamwork.

## For College Students & Grads
Competition is fierce. Here's how to stand out:
1.  **Projects Section**: Highlight class projects where you applied practical skills.
2.  **Internships**: Treat these as full-time jobs. Detail your accomplishments.
3.  **Leadership**: Were you a treasurer for a club? A team captain?

## Using the Right Tools
*   **Resume builder**: Tools like ApexResume have specific "Student" templates that handle low-experience layouts gracefully.
*   **Resume templates free download**: Good for inspiration, but ensure they are ATS friendly.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
*   **Too much fluff**: Keep it to one page.
*   **Unprofessional email**: Use `firstname.lastname@gmail.com`, not `coolguy123`.
*   **Typos**: Proofread twice.
